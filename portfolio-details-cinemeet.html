Let watch videos together during quarantine time!

The third-party desktop app, Cinemeet, aims to reconnect people distanced by COVID-19 by watching films and TV together. Through user research, we learned that users regard films/TVs as a way of deep communication and spending quality time together. Much as users want to watch a film/TV episode with their friends or family, they are concerned about choosing the right film that meet the tastes of everyone. Cinemeet will meet the needs of target audience, people who like movies/episodes and would like to build connections by watching together, by recommending films regarded great for watching, and providing cinema rooms that could be open for making new friends or private, shared only by people users are familiar with.
